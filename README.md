# Tarota AI

Tarota AI is an AI‑powered tarot reading platform that enables users to get instant, personalized, and private tarot readings online. Whether you seek guidance on love, career, life choices or spiritual growth, Tarota AI combines traditional tarot wisdom with AI‑driven interpretation to deliver meaningful insights — directly through the browser, without needing a physical deck or human reader. ([https://www.tarota.ai/](https://www.tarota.ai/))

## Overview

Tarota AI is built for people who want on‑demand tarot guidance, daily spiritual insights, or intuitive support for life’s questions. Users simply input a question or intention (about relationships, career, personal growth, etc.), choose a tarot spread (preset or custom), and receive a detailed AI‑powered reading. The platform aims to make tarot accessible, private, and easy to use — for both beginners and experienced users alike. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}
